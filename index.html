ABOUT US

Why Choose Us?

A Service With An Added Personal Touch

Our aim is to form lasting working relationships with our customers. Putting their style needs first. And being able to listen to their requests and add our own personal touch to the work we do to enhance their appearance.

We Have Experienced Staff

We have over 25 years of experience in the business of image consultancy, from personal stylists, to barbers who have studied in barbering college, it is all about meeting expectations through the service we provide.

Pre Booking Online

You can choose the day and the time that meets your preference, we understand that people have busy lives and therefore need to priortise things like haircuts and grooming. We have your needs at the front of our minds.

Affordable Cost

Competitive prices, however we are in touch with reality and our services won't break the bank, your paying for quality and there is a distinct difference in the service you will get at the Cutting Room as opposed to your other high street barber.
